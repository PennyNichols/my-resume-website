About Me

I come from a background in Biomedical Science, Dentistry, Neuroscience, Neuropsychology, and now Software Engineering. Although I have taken on many challenging fields in my life, I have excelled in each one. My deep commitment to everything I do is immensely evident in my achievements. I wasn't sure how I would do in Computer Science, as I barely knew how to use a computer when I began my studies at Clarusway. After beginning IT Fundamentals, I realized that contrary to popular opinion amongst Neuroscientists, computers are a lot like brains. The moment I started thinking of computer processes the same way I do about neurochemical processes, it all began to make sense, and I was able to take on far more than the assignments given to me. I branched out and studied front-end, back-end, local storage, session storage, database development, database connectivity, and a complete website deployment with domain routing before finishing IT fundamentals and beginning front-end studies with the class. I was even able to code a few simple games, per request of the kids in my life. I passed my HTML, CSS, and Javascript LinkedIn assessments in the top 5% of millions of people. I have gained many valuable skills in software development in the past year and continue to excel and learn additional skills, frameworks, and libraries on my own. I hope to one day have the opportunity to combine my deep-seated love for neuroscience with my new-found love for software development.
